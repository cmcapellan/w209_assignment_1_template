My Apple Watch Data: Weekly Active Calories

Source: Apple Health data downloaded via QS Access


This chart shows my active calories for each week as measured by my Apple Watch starting at the beginning of 2019 (the first week started on 12/30/2018) through the end of August 2020.

Apple defines "Active Calories" as calories burned while actively moving around such as when walking or exercising. This metric does not include calories from a person's Basal Metabolic Rate (which is based on age, gender, height and weight).

The key insight for me is the impact on my activity by the start of the pandemic in March 2020. I had big dropoff which I was able to rebound from in April, but then I've still trailed off in the summer of 2020.
